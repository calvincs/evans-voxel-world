## Swapping in your own

Replace any file here (`.mp3`/`.ogg`/`.wav`) and refresh the browser. The
"75 CC0 breaking / falling / hit" pack also has `wood_breaking`, `glass_breaking`,
and `metal_hit` variants if you want a different break sound; the steps pack has
`stone`, `wood`, `leaves`, and `mud` footsteps. More free options:

- https://opengameart.org  (filter License → "CC0")
- https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/  and  https://pixabay.com/music/
- https://kenney.nl/assets?q=audio  (CC0 game audio packs)
